### Stage Breakdown

**Stage 1 â€” Awareness**
The entry point. Where cold audiences first encounter the brand. Sources: organic SEO, paid ads, social content, referrals.

Design principle: *one clear message, one clear audience.* Broad messaging loses everyone.

**Stage 2 â€” Interest**
The capture stage. A visitor becomes a lead by exchanging their contact info for something genuinely valuable â€” a free audit, a guide, a template, a case study.

Design principle: *the lead magnet must solve a real problem immediately.* Generic PDFs don't convert in 2026.

**Stage 3 â€” Decision**
The trust stage. Email sequences, case studies, social proof, and retargeting ads work together to move a lead from "interested" to "ready to buy."

Design principle: *address every objection before the sales call happens.*

**Stage 4 â€” Action**
The conversion stage. A high-converting landing page with a frictionless CTA â€” call booking, checkout, or inquiry form â€” paired with an automated follow-up sequence that fires within minutes.

Design principle: *speed wins. First responder gets the client.*
